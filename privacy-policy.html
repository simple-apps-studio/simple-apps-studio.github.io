Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23rd January, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Pi Technologies (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Quick Estimate AI mobile application (“the App”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy describes what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Text inputs used to generate estimates (e.g., project details, descriptions).
  • Optional feedback or support messages you send to us.

These inputs are used only to generate estimates and improve app functionality.

b. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash and performance logs

This data helps us improve app stability and performance.

2. AI Processing

  • The App uses artificial intelligence to generate estimates based on the information you provide.
  • Input data may be processed by AI services to generate results.
  • We do not use your inputs to identify you personally.
  • We do not sell or share AI input data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Estimate data may be temporarily processed to generate results.
  • We do not permanently store estimate inputs unless explicitly stated in the app.
  • Data may be retained only as long as necessary to operate and improve the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Crash reporting
  • AI processing
  • Payment processing (for subscriptions or in-app purchases)

These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We only work with providers that follow standard data protection practices.

5. Payments and Subscriptions

  • All payments are processed securely through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  • We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Subscription management is handled by the respective app store.

6. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for tracking outside the App

Data may be disclosed only if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing

You can contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
